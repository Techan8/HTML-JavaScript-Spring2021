Maite Boaventura

My Bio

My name is Maite Boaventura, I was born October 12, 1987 in Paris, also known as "the city of love" in France. My parents originated from Cape Verde, they moved to France both at a young age. I am 33 years old, married and the happy mother of a beautiful little boy. I attended Louise Michel High School and graduated in 2006. In 2010 while on a trip to the US, I met my husband of whom I've been married to for almost 6 years. I moved to America about 5 years ago almost 6 now. Building my new life in Rhode Island after 27 years old was difficult at first, but with the support and love from my husband and his family, I was able to adjust quickly. The medical field was my first work experience in this country. I worked as a Certified Nursing Aid (CNA), then obtained a certificate to become a Certified Medical Technician (CMT) from August 2017 until January 2019. After having my son, I had the opportunity to work at Providence Community Health Centers (PCHC) as a Customer Service Representative (CSR). It was a great experience however, I wasn't interested enough to continue in this field. My husband encouraged me to go back to school into a field that I would be passionate about. This is how I decided to enroll into the ,Video Game Design and Development Program at New England Institute of Technology (NEIT).

My passion for video games started when I was a child. I used to play Super Mario Bros on GameBoy but everything changed when my father gifted me with a Playstation. My siblings and I played for hours in a day. I remember playing platform games like Crash Bandicoot and Spyro the dragon, fighting games like Tekken 3 etc. Back then, it was not common for a girl to play video games. At school I used to discuss with other classmates about video games and we used to exchange our games. One of my friend introduced me to Final Fantasy. I first played Final Fantasy VI and really enjoyed it, sparking my interest for RPGs. One day, I was offered to try Final Fantasy X on Playstation 2. The difference between both games was incredible. I was immediately seduced by the design and the innovative gameplay. Since then, I have been mostly playing RPGs as it is my favorite kind of gameplay.

Drawing has also always been part of my life. I started drawing Disney characters when I was 10 years old. I quickly escalated to manga and anime drawing style as I was discovering the comics. After high school I wanted to attend a private school to learn how to become a Graphic Design Comic Artist. Unfortunately, my parents couldn't afford the tuition, I had to abandon the idea of ever becoming a Comic Artist. Now that I am an adult, I have the possibility to fulfill my dream on a higher level by becoming a Video Game Artist. Therefore, I am starting my journey at New England Institute of Technology.
