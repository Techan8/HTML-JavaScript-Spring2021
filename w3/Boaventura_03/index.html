Bio

My name is Maite Boaventura, I was born October 12, 1987 in Paris, also known as "the city of love" in France. My parents originated from Cape Verde, they moved to France both at a young age. I am 33 years old, married and the happy mother of a beautiful little boy. I attended Louise Michel High School and graduated in 2006. In 2010 while on a trip to the US, I met my husband of whom I've been married to for almost 6 years. I moved to America about 5 years ago almost 6 now. Building my new life in Rhode Island after 27 years old was difficult at first, but with the support and love from my husband and his family, I was able to adjust quickly. The medical field was my first work experience in this country. I worked as a Certified Nursing Aid (CNA), then obtained a certificate to become a Certified Medical Technician (CMT) from August 2017 until January 2019. After having my son, I had the opportunity to work at Providence Community Health Centers (PCHC) as a Customer Service Representative (CSR). It was a great experience however, I wasn't interested enough to continue in this field. My husband encouraged me to go back to school into a field that I would be passionate about. This is how I decided to enroll into the ,Video Game Design and Development Program at New Institute of Technology (NEIT).
